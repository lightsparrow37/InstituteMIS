Institute Introduction

"Welcome to our educational institute, located in the beautiful town of Radhe Radhe in Bhaktapur, Nepal. Our institute is dedicated to providing high-quality education and training in the field of computer programming, including Python, C, C#, web development, ethical hacking, Linux, and Android development. At our institute, we strive to ensure that our students receive the most up-to-date training possible, based on the latest industry trends and demands. This means that our curriculum is constantly evolving to meet the needs of the job market, so that our students are well-equipped to find work after completing our courses.

We believe that the best way to learn is by doing, which is why we incorporate weekly projects into every course we offer. This gives our students hands-on experience and helps them build their development portfolios, which can be invaluable when looking for work in the field. We are proud of our dedicated and experienced team of instructors, who are passionate about helping our students succeed. We strive to create a supportive and collaborative learning environment where everyone feels welcome and valued.

Thank you for considering our institute for your education and training needs. We look forward to helping you achieve your goals and succeed in your chosen field." - Prakash Timilsina, CEO
